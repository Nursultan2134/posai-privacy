Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how PosAi ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with your use of the PosAi mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

The App may collect the following types of information:

Account Information: When you register, we collect your email address and password to authenticate your account.

Business Data: Product names, prices, sales records, and inventory data that you enter into the App. This data is stored securely and used solely to provide the App's functionality.

Camera and Photo Library: The App requests access to your camera and photo library solely for scanning QR codes and barcodes. We do not store or share any photos or camera data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

— Provide, operate, and maintain the App
— Authenticate users and manage access for owners and staff
— Display sales history, inventory, and reports within the App
— Improve and optimize the App's performance

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase cloud infrastructure. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not share your data with anyone except as necessary to operate the App (e.g., our cloud storage provider).

5. Camera and Photo Library Access

The App requests access to your device's camera and photo library for the purpose of scanning QR codes and barcodes. This permission is used only when you actively use the scanning feature. No images are stored or transmitted outside your device.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for business use by adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
